About Hydroponics...

Hydroponics is a method of growing plants in a water based, nutrient rich solution.This does not use soil, instead the root system is supported using an inert medium such as perlite, rockwool or clay pellets. In this project we have conducted a research on the difference in the growth of the tulsi plant under red and white lights. The system is open and fully automated. The temperature, humidity, flow rate and float sensors are used for controlling the system parameters. The readings are displayed on and the features are controlled using a dashboard.

Vision...

This soilless farming system provides better growth rate, pH control of the solution and nutrient efficiency. Also this uses 80% less water and fewer pesticides. The plants grown by this method are immune to pests and diseases. The purpose of hydroponics is to allow us to experiment and develop new techniques to further advance this agriculture field.
